Lasagna

Description

This lasanga dish combines italian sausage, ground beef, ricotta and mozzarella cheeses, and a homemade meat sauce. Serves 12.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Italian sausage
  • 3/4 lb. ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 2 cans tomate sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
  • salt to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • chopped parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In Dutch oven, cook sausage, beef, onion,and garlic over medium heat until well browned. Stir in the three tomato ingredients and water. Add sugar, basil, salt, pepper, and parsley. Simmer covered for 1 hour.

  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna in pot for 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta with egg, remaining parsley and 1/2 teaspoon of salt.

  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F

  4. To assemble, spread meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles over meatsauce. Spread half of ricotta mixture. Top with mozzarella slices. Spread meat sauce on top and sprinkle Parmesan cheese. Repeat layers and top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan. Cover with foil.

  5. Bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 25 minutes. Cool before serving.
